THE VILLAS

Cretan Villas are situated in the south and north Cretan coast, in the prefectures of Heraklion and Lassithi. Our villas are spread around the picturesque villages Kamilari, Kalamaki, Sivas and Listaros in the south, Gouves and Heraklion in the north and Agios Konstantinos in Lassithi Plateau.

Our guests will have the option to explore the beauty and history of the island, its unique nature and beaches and try diverse activities.
